I am a Ph.D. student in mathematics at the Université de Genève, advised by Prof. Hugo Duminil-Copin.

My research interests are in probability theory and statistical physics, especially critical and near-critical phenomena in planar percolation and random-cluster models.

Before coming to Geneva, I studied mathematics at the École Normale Supérieure, Université Paris-Saclay, and Peking University.

You can find my [CV](/cv/) and [publications](/publications/) on this website.
